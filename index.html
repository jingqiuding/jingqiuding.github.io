about

I am a postdoc in the Computer Science department at ETH Zurich. Before this, I got my computer science PhD at ETH Zurich, fortunately advised by David Steurer.

I work on computational complexity in machine learning problems, with a focus on robust and differentially private random graph estimation. In Fall 2024 I was fortunate to visit Sitan Chen.

research interests

  • Computational complexity in trustworthy machine learning.
  • Trustworthy knowledge production and propagation in the era of LLMs.
